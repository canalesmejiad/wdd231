DC Electric - Individual Project Site Plan

WDD 231 · Site Plan

Site Name

Proposed Site Name: DC Electric - Reliable Electrical Services

This name represents a small electrical company that offers professional and safe electrical services for homes and small businesses in Vancouver, Washington.

Example domain (not required to be real): dcelectricvancouver.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to show basic information about DC Electric, including:

  • Main electrical services (residential and small commercial).
  • Service area in Vancouver, WA.
  • Contact information and a simple form to request a quote.
  • Short safety tips for homeowners.

Scenarios

Examples of questions that visitors might have when they visit the site:

  • Scenario 1: “My house has some outlets that don’t work. How can I contact DC Electric to schedule a visit?”
  • Scenario 2: “Does DC Electric install new lights and outlets for a small home remodel?”
  • Scenario 3: “What areas of Vancouver, WA does DC Electric serve?”

Color Scheme

The site will use a simple color scheme to keep it clean and easy to read.

Primary Color

Hex: #0B3C5D
Used for: headings, header background, and some borders.

Accent Color

Hex: #FFC107
Used for: buttons, important links, and small highlights.
