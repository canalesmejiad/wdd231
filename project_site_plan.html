DC Electric – Individual Project Site Plan

WDD 231 • Site Plan

Site Name

Proposed Site Name: DC Electric – Reliable Electrical Services

This reflects a small electrical company offering safe and professional electrical services in Vancouver, Washington.

Site Purpose

The website will:

  • Show main residential and small commercial electrical services.
  • Display the service area in Vancouver, WA.
  • Provide contact information and a quote form.
  • Share basic electrical safety tips.

Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: “How do I contact DC Electric to schedule a repair?”
  • Scenario 2: “Does DC Electric install lighting for remodels?”
  • Scenario 3: “What areas of Vancouver, WA are included?”
